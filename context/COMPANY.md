# Company

## What is this company?

Restacked.ai is a context orchestration platform for teams building with AI.

It solves the core problem of AI sessions starting from zero by making the right

context available to every AI tool automatically, structured and versioned in GitHub.

## What do we sell and to whom?

Restacked.ai sells context orchestration infrastructure to vibe coding consultants

and small development teams building production B2B platforms for SMB clients.

## Who do we build for?

Three personas:

- Persona 1: Vibe coding consultant building B2B platforms for SMB clients
- Persona 2: Dev team CEO scaling a software delivery agency
- Persona 3: SMB platform owner who depends on the delivered platform

## What is the core insight?

Having context is not the solution. Context orchestration is.

Getting the right context to the right AI tool at the right moment,

generated from live sources, translated by humans where needed,

enforced at the gateway layer.
